Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 1, 2026  ·  Last Updated: March 1, 2026
Short version: deeltrack currently stores all your data locally in your browser. We do not operate servers that collect your deal or investor data. As we add cloud features, this policy will be updated and you will be notified.

1. Who We Are

deeltrack ("we," "us," "our") operates the deeltrack platform at deeltrack.com. We are a software company providing organizational tools for real estate syndicators. We are not a financial institution, investment adviser, or broker-dealer.

2. What Data We Collect

2.1 Data You Enter Into the Platform

All deal information, investor records, financial projections, distribution history, and generated documents that you enter into deeltrack are stored in your browser's localStorage — on your device, not our servers. This includes:

  • Deal names, terms, financial projections, property addresses
  • Investor names, email addresses, phone numbers, accreditation status
  • Distribution amounts and history
  • Capital call records
  • Generated document content
  • Firm settings and profile information

2.2 Account Data

When you create an account (email and password), this credential is stored in your browser's localStorage. We do not currently operate an authentication server.

2.3 Usage Data (Future)

As we introduce cloud features, we may collect: page views, feature usage events, session duration, browser type and version, and IP address for security purposes. We will update this policy and notify users before any server-side data collection begins.

4. Data Sharing

We do not currently sell, rent, or share your data with third parties because we do not receive it on our servers. The following third-party services may process limited data:

  • OpenStreetMap / Nominatim: When you use the portfolio map, property location strings are sent to Nominatim's geocoding API to determine coordinates. No personal investor data is sent.
  • Google Fonts / Font Awesome CDN: These services may log your IP address when loading fonts and icons. This is standard for CDN usage.
  • GitHub Pages: Our hosting provider. Standard web server logs (IP, page requested, timestamp) may be retained by GitHub per their privacy policy.

5. Investor Data — Special Obligations

If you use deeltrack to store information about your investors (names, emails, financial information, accreditation status), you are acting as the data controller for that information. You are responsible for:

  • Obtaining appropriate consent from investors to store their information;
  • Complying with applicable data protection laws (CCPA, GDPR if applicable);
  • Securing the device on which this data is stored;
  • Deleting investor data when no longer needed or upon investor request.

We recommend using the Export and Clear Data features in Settings to manage data retention.

6. Data Security

Because your data is stored locally, its security depends on the security of your device and browser. We recommend:

  • Using a strong device password or screen lock;
  • Not using deeltrack on shared or public computers;
  • Regularly exporting data backups (Settings → Export All);
  • Using the Export feature before clearing browser data.

7. California Residents (CCPA)

California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected, to delete personal information, and to opt-out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information. Since data is stored on your device, you can delete it at any time via Settings → Danger Zone → Reset All Data, or by clearing your browser's localStorage.

8. Children's Privacy

deeltrack is not directed to individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We will post changes to this policy on this page with an updated effective date. If we introduce server-side data collection, we will notify all registered users by email at least 30 days in advance.
