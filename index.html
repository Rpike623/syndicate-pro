Not Just Tracking — Generating

We build the documents your deals need.

Most platforms make you upload everything yourself. Deeltrack actually creates the operating agreements, K-1s, investor statements, and offering memos your syndication requires.

Operating Agreements

Generate state-specific OAs with proper waterfall language, capital account provisions, and GP/LP rights. Covers TX, CA, NY, FL, DE, and more — ready for your attorney's review.

K-1 Tax Documents

Produce Schedule K-1 (Form 1065) allocations for every LP automatically. Income, deductions, distributions, and capital accounts — calculated and formatted for tax season.

Waterfall Calculations

Define your pref rate, catch-up, and promote tiers once. Deeltrack computes every distribution split — preferred returns, GP catch-up, and residual splits — down to the penny.

Offering Memorandums

Build professional OMs with live-preview editing. Cover page, investment highlights, market analysis, financial projections, deal structure, team bios, and risk factors — export to PDF.

Capital Account Statements

Generate per-investor capital account statements with contributions, distributions, running balances, and YTD performance — ready to send or publish to the LP portal.

Investor Updates & Notices

Create quarterly updates, distribution notices, and capital call letters with real deal data baked in. KPI snapshots, CapEx progress, and outlook sections — all auto-populated.

Full Platform

Everything else you need to run your syndication.

Deal Pipeline

Investor CRM
