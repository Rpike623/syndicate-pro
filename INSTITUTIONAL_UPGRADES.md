Deploying the final 5 institutional gears to make deeltrack an enterprise fund management powerhouse.

- Path: reit-dashboard.html

## 2. Automated K-1 Distribution Engine
- Integration: SP-Email Bridge.
- Feature: Instead of individually emailing K-1s, the GP now has a "Broadcast Tax Docs" button that delivers secure, role-scoped links to 100% of the LP base in one pass.

## 3. LP Survey & ESG Integration
- Path: lp-survey.html
- Feature: Capture "Investment Profiles" (Risk Tolerance, Property Preferences) directly from LPs. Data writes to the 'Appetite' chart in Investor Sentiment.

## 4. Property Health Monitoring (Telemetry)
- Feature: Live Occupancy and Collections hooks in Deal Detail.
- Benefit: Allows GPs to show "Live Asset Performance" rather than quarterly static snapshots.

## 5. Master White-label Settings
