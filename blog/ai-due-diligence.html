What Makes It Smart

The AI doesn't just dump a generic list. It considers:

  • Property type — multifamily DD is different from industrial DD is different from retail DD
  • Vintage — a 1970s property needs different physical inspections than a 2015 build
  • Deal size — a $3M duplex has proportionally different DD needs than a $50M portfolio
  • Location — flood zones, seismic zones, environmental history vary by geography

Critical Items

Each checklist item is marked as critical or standard. Critical items are non-negotiable — things like Phase I Environmental, title search, lender-required inspections, and T-12 financial review. Missing a critical item can kill a deal or expose you to liability.

Standard items are important but may be scoped based on the deal: full unit-by-unit inspection for a 50-unit property makes sense; for a 500-unit portfolio, you'd sample.

Estimated Timeline

The AI provides an estimated total DD timeline based on the checklist scope — typically 30-60 days for a standard multifamily acquisition. This helps you negotiate the DD period in your PSA with a realistic timeframe instead of guessing.

Using the Checklist

The generated checklist is interactive — you can check off items as they're completed, add notes, and track progress. It becomes a living DD tracker for the deal, not a static PDF you lose in your downloads folder.

First-time sponsors:

If you've never done a commercial acquisition, the DD checklist is invaluable. It tells you what to ask for, what to inspect, and what to verify — compiled from the experience of operators who've closed hundreds of deals. It's like having a mentor hand you their personal checklist.

From any deal profile, click the AI Tools menu and select "Due Diligence Checklist." The AI generates the checklist in about 20 seconds. You can regenerate it if you update the deal details (e.g., changing property type from multifamily to mixed-use).
